Library Policies & Rules

Please familiarize yourself with our comprehensive library policies and rules to ensure a pleasant and productive experience for all patrons. Adherence helps us maintain a respectful and conducive environment for everyone's academic pursuits.

Borrowing Policy

Students may borrow up to 5 books for 14 days. Faculty members are eligible for up to 10 books for 30 days. A valid BHC ID is required for all borrowing transactions.

Renewal Policy

Books can be renewed a maximum of two times, provided the request is made before the original due date and the item has not been reserved by another patron.

Overdue Fines

A fee of ‚Ç±5.00 per day will be charged for each overdue book, up to a maximum of ‚Ç±100.00 per book. Prompt returns are highly appreciated to avoid incurring fines.

Reference Materials

Reference books, academic journals, and special collections are strictly designated for in-library use only and are not available for circulation or removal from the premises.

Quiet Zones

Designated quiet study areas require absolute silence. All mobile devices must be turned off or silenced, and phone calls should be conducted outside the library. Collaborative study rooms are available for group discussions.

Computer Usage

Library computer sessions are limited to 2 hours. Patrons must sign up at the circulation desk with a valid student or faculty ID. Priority will be given to academic research and study purposes.

Food & Drinks

Only clear water in sealed, spill-proof bottles is permitted within the library's premises. Food and other beverages are strictly prohibited to prevent damage to valuable library materials and equipment.

Personal Belongings

Large bags, backpacks, and other personal items should be stored in the provided lockers at the library entrance. The BHC Library is not responsible for lost, damaged, or stolen personal belongings.

Digital Resources

Access to our extensive collection of e-books, online journals, and research databases is available to all patrons with a valid BHC Library ID. Remote access details and guides can be found on our official website.
